    MY MOTIVATION FOR CHOOSING THE HBO-ICT PROGRAMME

    Technology was introduced to me at a tender age. With technology evolving rapidly, the world requires computer specialists because they are an integral part of our daily lives as it is always in demand.

    As a result, this has really convinced me to pursue a career in ICT because it helps to improve productivity, output and efficiency. I am also genuinely interested in learning ICT because it allows me to learn new things such as: web development, computer programming, hardware skills and collaborating online for work and feedback and adapting to new things because ICT is always growing and changing. In the future I aspire to be either a Software Engineer, Business IT Consultant or even a Data Scientist.

    Another reason why I am motivated for this study program is that it will improve my personal development such as:

    • enhancing my critical and analytical thinking skills, that I believe, will aid me in solving challenging problems.
    • exhibiting my creativity
    • making the right decisions
    • remaining open-minded

    Given the fantastic career opportunities avaliable in the ICT field, I would love to graduate with my ICT degree and work in any part of the world.
