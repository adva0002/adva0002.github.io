FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

To print a document from your laptop at HZ, navigate to the HZ Print Page first. Fill in all of your credentials and choose the file(s) you want to upload. After selecting the files, you can go and check out the additional options in settings and see if everything is in order. You then have the option to confirm your request. Then using your student card and the printing option, you can retrieve your document from the printer which is located on every corridor.
Simply follow all of the commands given on the website to scan a document and send it your laptop at HZ.
Navigate to the Webshop Page and sign in with your credentials. Once that is successful, you are then free to select or enroll in any event and can even purchase a ticket.
